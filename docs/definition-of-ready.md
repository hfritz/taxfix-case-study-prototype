# Definition Of Ready

A piece of work is ready to build when:

- The target user is clear.
- The problem is specific.
- Goals and non-goals are written down.
- The expected user experience is described.
- Success metrics or learning goals are defined.
- Major assumptions and risks are visible.
- The technical approach is clear enough to start.
- Verification expectations are known.

For small work, this can be a short checklist. For major work, it should be captured in product and technical specs.
