# Skill: AI Collaboration

## Use When

Using AI tools to accelerate product or engineering work.

## Workflow

1. Give the AI the relevant spec, constraints, and expected output.
2. Assign a focused role such as product agent, engineering agent, reviewer, or QA agent.
3. Ask for assumptions and risks explicitly.
4. Use AI to generate options, then apply human judgment.
5. Ask a separate reviewer pass for important work.
6. Verify generated implementation and claims.
7. Record meaningful decisions.

## Output

Clear AI-assisted work with visible assumptions, traceable decisions, and verified results.

## Quality Check

AI should increase speed and quality without making ownership blurry.
