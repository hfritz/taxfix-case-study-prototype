# Product Principles

These principles encode the product judgment this foundation repo should carry into every new project.

## Start With The Customer Problem

A feature is only valuable if it changes user behavior or business outcomes. Begin with the user, context, pain, motivation, and current workaround before describing the solution.

## Make Impact Measurable

Every meaningful effort should define:

- The outcome it is trying to move.
- The input metrics that explain progress.
- Guardrails that protect against harmful side effects.
- The learning expected from the work.

## Prefer Simple Systems That Scale

Good product work often removes complexity rather than adding surface area. Prefer solutions that make the user experience simpler and the operating model clearer.

## Build For Feedback Loops

Favor small releases, observable behavior, and fast learning over large hidden bets. The sooner reality responds, the better the product gets.

## Separate Goals From Bets

Goals describe the outcome. Bets describe how we believe we can reach it. Do not confuse commitment to an outcome with commitment to a specific feature.

## Understand The System

For marketplace, platform, SaaS, or AI products, optimize for system health rather than isolated local wins. Consider users, operators, supply, demand, incentives, cost, and trust.

## Keep Strategy Close To Execution

Strategy should shape what gets built, and execution should update strategy. Plans are useful only when they survive contact with users, data, and technical reality.

## AI Is A Product Multiplier

AI can accelerate research, prototyping, analysis, and implementation. Use it aggressively, but keep human judgment at the center of product direction, ethics, and quality.
