# AI Collaboration Principles

## Use AI For Leverage

Use AI to draft, critique, prototype, analyze, test, and explain. Keep humans responsible for direction, judgment, and accountability.

## Give AI Context And Constraints

The quality of output depends on the quality of input. Provide specs, goals, non-goals, examples, and known risks.

## Separate Generation From Review

For important work, use one pass to generate and another pass to critique. Different roles catch different problems.

## Verify Claims

AI-generated code, metrics, facts, and recommendations should be checked against the actual system or credible sources.

## Preserve Decision Memory

Use specs and decision records so AI agents can understand why the project is shaped the way it is.
